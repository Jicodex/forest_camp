WHAT WILL YOUR KIDS LEARN?

Forest Campers

Designed for kids ages 7-12, this adventure‐filled camp from 9am to 4pm offers the perfect blend of fun and learning in the great outdoors.

Participants will enjoy thrilling mountain sports, heartwarming interactions with animals, and immersive nature‐based activities at Scape Park.

Leadership Development

Participants ages 13 to 15 will accompany the Forest Camper Program with a role that requires more responsibility and patience.

The goal is to stretch their abilities to their limits for increased self awareness and guide younger campers by example.

OBJECTIVES

Our main goal is to pass on wilderness knowledge and survival techniques to help children develop cognitive skills, leadership traits and executive function.

FLORA & FAUNA

Time in the forest comes with chances to engage with wildlife—playing with monkeys, feeding iguanas—and exploring lakes & the ocean.

PLAY

Ludic activities in natural spaces let learning happen effortlessly, fostering non‐linear thinking and well‐being.

NUTRITION

Most foods are home‐made; processed foods are scarce. Sodas and fried foods are prohibited, following Stanford’s 2022 child‐nutrition standards.
